This is a transcription of the 1951 Journal of Roderick William Virgo. This hard blue covered book is hard to read, because of the quality of Dad's penmanship and because he wrote in pencil - sometimes very lightly with a hard pencil.

Saturday January 20, 1951

Dody home today! Everything very wonderful. She seems to be completely recovered.

Spent all afternoon with her father + mine cleaning up debris around our house. Found many interesting things. Dody worked for awhile in the house. Went to Vaughns + played Canasta. Had fun.

Sunday January 21, 1951

A nice day. Took Barb + Aunt Lena to church. Nailed a board on our house. Dody + Dick stayed home + got dinner. Had French fries. A nice lazy afternoon. Back to hospital at 6:30 PM

Monday January 22, 1951

Another School day!

Everything seems wonderful to happy now that Dody is well.

Tuesday January 23, 1951

Vet Class

Wish I could bring Dody home for good tomorrow.

Wednesday January 24, 1951

Last day of tests. Went to hospital in afternoon. Dody + I went to Utica to window shop. Had fun. Don't feel that she was as well today as this weekend. This will probably be my last trip to the Hosp. On Wed. for two reasons. (1) Ernest is leaving me to go back to school (2) Dody will be home soon.E

Saturday January 27, 1951

Went to Hospital for Dody but found she had a bad night + can't come home for a long time. Wish I knew how long. Had a nice weekend planned. Everything seems so lonesome + lost. There seems to be no future in things ever again.

Dad + I hauled a load of debris from the house. Awfully cold. the kids + I are going to stay at my mothers tonight - after that I just don't know. I wish Dody had stayed well + strong. I love her + need her. Baazar at school.

Sunday January 28, 1951

Kids + I came down took baths, washed hauled out ashes. Went to Church. Shed many tears stayed for Sun. School. Ate at Dick + Warenas. Bob + George + family there. Went down to our house. Went to Fabius for Aunt Lena. She is going to stay one more week. Then what?

Monday January 29, 1951

A usual school day. Faculty standards committee meeting after school. Bud Bowman + Ray Tuttle were at the Lincklean house for supper. Visited with them awhile. Mr. Albu - Mrs. Lynn Holmes + I went to ag. teachers meeting at Madison. Awfully cold. Am tired + lonesome tonight.

Tuesday January 30, 1951

Took Phil to Dr. for a shot. Took Dick to get haircut. Phil cried a lot. Guess he recognized Dr. Office. I love the little guy.

Aunt Lena was at Gr. Kates Barbie at Gr. Virgo's. Gathered everyone up + came home.

Vet. Class until 11:00 PM

Below zero tonight.

Wonder how Dody is. Very hard not to know.

Wednesday January 31, 1951

First Wed in 4 mo. That I have not been with Dody. Miss her very much.

After school snow carnival committee met to plan assembly program.

Went to Gr. Virgo's to help Aunt Catherine with her income tax.

Stop in Dentist + paid Dicks bill of 12.

N? meetings tonight. About 9:00PM phone rang + it was Paul + Clara Conine from Aberdine S.D. Had received my letter about Dody + wanted to know about her. I cried my eyes out wishing they were here + I knew how Dody is. Guess I'll go to bed early.

Got 2 tons of coal today.

Sunday February 4, 1951

Went to church - ate diner at Moms + then went down to the hospital - Dody has been put in C Bldg. not for long I hope. after seeing her I went to see Dr. Warner. Didn't get much satisfaction.

Monday February 5, 1951

Faculty meeting after school.

Barb went to scout meeting.

Tuesday February 6, 1951

Vet. Class

Wednesday February 7, 1951

Rained + snowed all day. No School. I worked at school ate dinner with my Mom. Kids stayed with Gr. Warena. I ate supper up there. Helped put rubber pads on stairs + replaced the carpet

awfully stormy when kids + I came home.

Worked on Empire farmer awards till bed time.

Thursday February 8, 1951

Very stormy today - no school. Took Barb up to Gr. Grimes as he is going to take her to dentist in Fayettville to have her teeth checked while he has an appointment.

Dick + I home alone all day. Just lazy.

Friday February 9, 1951

No school today

Saturday February 10, 1951

Cleaned house all morning. Worked at school all afternoon. Had supper at my folks.

Sunday February 11, 1951

Church - Dinner + mothers - P??ls to Grimes for dinner then to my folks for TV. - Me to Hospital - found Dody as well as could be expected + discouraged + unhappy! Breaks my heart to see her this way would do anything to help. If I only knew what to do

Talked to Dr. Goldbock - she said Dody was better + would call Dr. Ano + see if she could get us back in "A" bldg. for insulin treatments. Went from there to see Dr. Howard. He said he would talk to Dr. Goldbock Tues. about Dody + insulin treatment. Home - Bob + Geo. + family at Dodys folks Glenn, Jim + families at mine. Had supper - showed movies of Xmas.

Monday February 12, 1951

Worked on house all day - cleaned out libr - hauled away books - Grimes hot water heater went to pieces - ordered a new gas one. Dick has a little cold.

Tuesday February 13, 1951

Vet. Class.

Barb to scouts.

Wednesday February 14, 1951

Advisory Board meeting. Recommended Herby Jones - Ned Holmes = Philip Rynn for Empire farmer degree.

Thursday February 15, 1951

Glenn got orders to report back to Navy. Goes in Fort Niagra in 7 days - then must be in Calif in 30 days. He is mad + upset. Doesn't know what he is going to do with his family.

Friday February 16, 1951

Got 82 24 refund from Income tax. Just what I needed.

Took kids to Onedia basketball game at school. I took tickets. Barb loved the game + Dick OK until his head + stomach started aching. Noise to much for him.

Grumpy when we got home.

Saturday February 17, 1951

Cleaned, did washing, changed beds. Worked on house most of morning. Reynolds + I worked until 7:00PM. We got all the rafters back up, with roof boards on + roofing paper on south side. It rained all day + I was soaked + freezing cold.

Had supper at Mom's. Took pictures of Phil.

Barb has had a Toni today. She looks cute.

Sunday February 18, 1951

Kids + I to church - Dinner at Mom's. Mrs Partridge + I to hospital. Dody a little better but along ways from a month ago. Fairly cheerful. Drs say she is gaining. Is getting electric shock every Tues. + fri now. Must stay in C building for these treatments.

Monday February 19, 1951

F.F.A. meeting at 7:30 PM. Played basketball + had initiation.

Worked in rain after school to get roof fixed on house.

Home alone at 10:30. Kids at Gr. Warena's.

Tuesday February 20, 1951

Vet class.

Again alone at home.

After school went to Chittenango + traded enlarger + pistol for better enlarger.

Wednesday February 21, 1951

Nothing new today. Hard to get thru day with out going to see Dody. Am going one of these days.

Printed pictures after supper.

Thursday February 22, 1951

Nothing different. Tired out.

Phil has rash on face but think it is teeth.

Printed pictures.

Friday February 23, 1951

Pay day

Kids + I came home after school. They went over to "barn" for awhile. I washed car + got supper. Had French fries, fried eggs + beans think I shall bring kids home every Fri like this. It gives us a chance to be together alone.

After dishes - Lone Ranger + then I read to them for awhile.

Have made out this months checks.

Saturday February 24, 1951

Went to Syracuse to Dairynews League meeting. Took Dan Dorlan - Toni Westfall - Janis Morris.

Bought me a pair of pants 10.50 a Lexxxxries.

Sunday February 25, 1951

Church. My folks for diner. Went to see Dody. She didn't feel very well. Breaks my heart to see her like this. If only I could do something. Drs. say electric shock should lift her depression.

Monday February 26, 1951

Ordinary School day.

Agr. Teachers meeting in Morrisville - met at state school.

Traded Cameras with Joe Smith in Chittenango. Traded my 4x5 + little 127 camera + 3 Jap rifles for a reflex camera.

Traded pair of 7 power binoculars for flash attachment - film + flash bulbs.

Tuesday February 27, 1951

Vet. Class

Wednesday February 28, 1951

Church supper. Feel lousy. Am coming down with cold. Mrs. Flannigan showed pictures of her trip to the Vatican + Europe.

Thursday March 1, 1951

No school because of bad weather.

Kids + I went to Syracuse in afternoon. Got a key for school made. Bought Barbie a Browny suit. (5.50)(dress - socks + hat)

Saw Bobbie + George - everyone has colds.

Saw Jim at gas station. Glenn + I went to store buy some film

Friday March 2, 1951

Had supper at my folks.

Really got a cold. -

Took some pictures of Phil. He is a cute little guy.

Saturday March 3, 1951

Didn't do much but work around house. Feel terrible. Slept most of afternoon. Went up town got hair cut got some groceries.

got two meals today - whew, do I ever appreciate a womans job.

Sunday March 4

No church because electricity was off. Down to see Dody. She felt alittle better. Still confused about what it is all about. Drs. say they are going to start her on insulin in A Building. Awfully glad she is being moved back. This means she gets started 6 wks. earlier - also she will be home six weeks sooner. We lover her and need her.

Glenn + Susy were out today. They are going to put their furniture in our barn while they go to California.

Monday

Bad cold - but went to school anyway about 150 kids out because of I???. went to Bobbi + Georges + got Barbs scout dress that they exchanged for us. Stayed for supper.

[this is outlined in black pen and red pencil] Dody sarts insulin treatment.

Sunday March 11, 1951

Church as usual-

Lynn - Susie + I went to see Dody - She was in A Bldg. Didn't say much. I was glad to see her moved.

Monday

School - nothing of importance.

March 13

No Vet. Class.

Saturday March 17

Worked all afternoon + until 12:00 midnight put in new sink at Grimes. Everything went pretty good.

Folks went to W. Moores after supper.

Sunday March 18, 1951

To church same as usual with kids - Went to hospital to see Dody - she has not been well + was put in C bldg for day. She refused to see me - I felt terrible but left - Dr. Ano asked me to find out more about her illness 10 yrs. ago. He said in 2 weeks he could tell me more about her.

It is hard to understand all this but there must be a reason.

Monday March 19

School

Tuesday March 20

All day in Farm + Home week of Cornell Left 9:00 AM arrived 11:30AM.

Took Tom Barden - Eugene Masutl - Ned Holmes - Harlen Jones - Robl. Dorranie - Bill Prihards - David Shepard.

I went to Kermis Show in evening - Boys to wrestling matches -

Boys up until 4:00 A.M. Pretty noisy!

Wednesday March 21

Got boys up a 6:00 AM. They didnt get much rest (nor I).

Spent most of day showing boys around campus.

Harlen + Ned went home with Mrs. Cook.

We left at about 4:00 P.M.

Key locked in car, had to disconnect wires + hook together to get home. Got home at about 6:30 P.M..

Took Barb + Dick to gym demonstration. They liked it very much.

Thursday, March 22, 1951

Easter Vacation starts.

Worked at school in morning.

Spent afternoon in Syracuse we to hospital (city) asked them to send transcript of Dodys 11yr ago illness to hospital. Tried to find Dr. Silverman but could only get him on phone. He said he would send a report to hospital.

Did a little shopping.

Friday March 23

Went to Perryville -

Called on Archie McEvers Daneheys - R. Smith - W. Christenson.

March 24, 1951 - Saturday

Cleaned house in the morning.

Moved some furniture out of our house.

Helped Dick finish sink job in his house.

March 25, 1951 - Sunday

Easter Sunday - Lots of people in church. Bob + Geo. were there. Went to hospital - found Dody in bed under affects of (Amital (sp) Thinth seram). Visited pretty good but still isn't vey well. Does not believe that I or anyone is real. Seems to think only of not wanting to live. I wish so mush that she would again want to get well.

Burke sisters are on first floor. That means they are nearly well. I am glad for them.

Lynn + Jim + Bobbie out home.

Mon[day]

No school - Easter vacation.

Called in Erieville this morning - saw Phil Pynn - Roger Stawring - Lee Tainter = Robt. Morris - Tom Westfall.

Worked at school all afternoon. Kids + I home for supper - they went over to barn.

March 27, Tuesday

School started

Vet class

April 1, 1951 - Sunday

Church - Hospital - Dody not to well but Dr. Ano (sp) says she will get better. I know that but when -

April 2, 1951 Monday

Warrena + Bard + I went to city after school to shop. Got Barb 2 dress - sweater + spring coat.

April 3 - Tuesday

Vet class

April 4 Wednesday

Lynn + Jim moved out to care for Unk + Phil while folks are in Florida.

April 5

My folks left for a month in Florida.

April 6 Friday

Got grippe bug today.

Didn't get much rest all night.

April 7, 1951 Saturday

Feel terrible - Dick _ I hauled trailer load of junk from around home to dump.

Went to Dr. Raymond felt louzy but I got a Lumber Co truck + Reynolds + I hauled 2 loads more from house.

Dick did not go to school all week. Virus infection.

April 8 Sunday

Kids + I went to church. Went to hospital to see Dody. I believe she was some better. We had a nice visit.

April 9 Monday

Went to school feeling rather sick. Left at noon. Slept all afternoon + night.

Kids at Grma Warenas

April 10 Tuesday

Stayed home all day. Awfully lonesome.

No vet class

To Grma Warenas for supper.

April 11, 1951

Feel louzy but went to school - to lonesome home.

April 12 Thursday

Feel better.

Went with Barbie to Scout Father + Daughter banquet.

Barb + I at home for night.

April 13 Friday

Career day at school

April 14 Saturday

Chuck _ I worked at "our" house during morning. Now have it cleaned back to bathroom wall.

Chuck + his Dad went to Cornell to get Dave Bacon. They saw a lacrosse game.

Kids + I were lazy + came home + slept in afternoon. Kids went to movies (Roy Rogers).

April 15, Sunday

Church with kids - Chuck _ Dad there. Went to hospital. Found Dody much better than for last month. She looked at old photos with me. I know she is going to get well. I lover her so much.

April 17, Tuesday

Vet Class.

April 18, Wednesday

Ag. Teachers meeting in DeRuyter took f. Townsend - xxx Jones with me. Good meeting. Dr. Bralton of Cornell spoke on Types of gov't programs that affect Agr.

April 19, Thursday

Helped Jim move some furniture from Syracuse.

April 20 Friday

Showed some movies at Homer Shepards for a 4-H club meeting.

April 21 Saturday

Worked at our house for the morning - helped Jim finish moving in the afternoon.

Chuck back to Texas at midnight.

April 22, Sunday

Church with kids. Diner with Lynn + Jim. Hospital to see Dody. This was a bad Sun. I had high hopes of finder her better. Its kinda hard to take.

April 23, Monday

School as usual.

April 24, Tuesday

Phils birthday - he has some teeth - can walk - patty cake - wave good bye. Lynn cooked a nice big choc. cake. He received a shirt + socks from Lynn + Jim - + a sweater from Gr Warena.

No vet class tonight. Home with kids.

After school I went to Morrisville + got zoro trees for Ray - Mayer + Al Phoft.

April 26, Thursday

Faculty meeting. Mr Rice spoke + succeeded making me very mad. I spoke my piece + wish I hadn't (of course).

April 27, Friday

Pay day-

Mr. Lathinen from state Department here - talked with Phil - Harlin - Ned about Empire farmer award. Harlin + Ned were accepted.

Junior Prom -

Contracts out - mine $4000 for next year - a $200 raise.

Took Phil + visited Grandma Kate for a while.

April 28 Saturday

Went to Mayers with trees.

Took Reynolds motor boat to Kinards for repair + painting.

Took old bathroom out of our house - Dan Delronie was going to come + look at it but it rained all afternoon.

Dick + I got hair cuts. Ate with Jim + Lynn.

April 29 Sunday

Church with kids. Diner with Jim + Lynn. Hosp. to see Dody. She felt pretty good, had nice visit - took photo album down - Has been sick with cold missed 2 treatments [2 treatments is outlined] Dr. Ano says she is getting better. He had a letter from Dr. Silverman about Dody's sickness 11 yrs. ago. They admitted that they never knew what was the mater with her. Makes me + her folks awfully "mad".

Ate supper with Lynn + Jim. Jim had call from Syracuse saying some one had stolen 2 tires off his trailer. He + went out + checked + sure enough - reported it to the police - He doesn't know whether they are insured or not. what a guy!!

Barb has sore throat.

Monday

Worked all day at house = got it pretty well cleaned up - Lots of work yet. Took carpets out to dry, will send them to Syracuse to be cleaned.

May 1 Tuesday

Vet class

Phil came down with Chicken Pox - has it good.

May 2 Wednesday

Advisory board meeting.

May 3, Thursday

Made alot of calls on boys.

May 4, Friday

Call on boys during morning.

Worked on house in afternoon.

Mom + Dad started home from Florida.

Phil points + says "see! Poor little guy sure is spotted.

May 5, Saturday

Took kids to Syracuse - got Dick 1 pr dung. 2 pr. school pants - socks - Barbie - dress + socks - me underwear. Worked on house in afternoon.

May 6, Sunday

Church with kids - ate with Lynn + Jim. Hospital to see Dody. Had Amitol + felt pretty good. Dr. Nasholdt says I must commit her because she is not as well as should. Broke my heart cause I thought we were gaining slowly. They are stopping Insulin treatments as they are producing any results. Are going to try some sleeping treatment now.

Felt terrible on the way home. Didn't much care whether I ever got got there. Had long talk with Dodys folks. Don't know what to do about committing her - certainly don't want to.

Mon.

School as usual. Faculty meeting after school - we are going to get new contracts with more money!

When to see Dr. Becouson (sp). He advised me to go see Dr. Streckil in Syracuse about my problem.

Did big wash for Lynn after supper.

No vet. class.

Phone Dr. Raiffaile + Dr. Stecke today. Can't get appointment with the first + couldn't get Dr. Stechel on the phone.

Dan Delorme started on our house today. Hope I can pay for all this!

All Dodys folks plus Tip plus Jim helped me move furniture out of house tonight. It sure was a big help.

May 9 Wednesday

Morrisville - county F.F.A. meeting.

May 10 Thursday

School as usual.

Warena _ I went to Syracuse to see Dr. Steckel in state tower Bldg. We wanted some help in deciding what to do with Dody. He advised that we commit her. He felt that the hospital was doing everything possible for her.

I still don't know what to do.

May 11 Friday

Took 40 agr. boys to Syracuse to the Dairynews League Pasturizing + bottling works.

Mom + Dad got home from Florida - Both are tanned.

Jim helped me at house till dark.

May 12 Saturday

Worked all day in house. Capenters put in new joists in cellar. Payed Dan Dilorine $130 for labor on house.

Ate with my folks.

May 13 Sunday

Mothers Day

Curch with kids. Diner with my folks. Hospital with Dody - She felt pretty good. Had nice visit - she had had Amitol. Took down blouse the Mom got in Florida. She liked it but I brought it back as she had no skirt to wear it with. The Drs. have not yet started her sleep treatment. Can't get medicine.

May 19 Saturday

Soph. - Seni private -

Took our big radio up + used it in gym Mr. + Mrs. Gleason chaparons with me. Missed Dody very much.

May 20 Sunday

Church. Hospital - Dody feeling quite well - took her a new dress.

May 21 Monday

Dody started sleep treatment - didn't know until Sun May 27th

May 24 Thursday

Took - Dave Shephard - Edmund Holmes - Phil Ryun - Harlen Jones - Marvin Danon to Malone, N.Y. for state F.F.A. convention. Left Caz at 8:00 AM - arrived 1:15 P.M. registered - ate then went up tp Cazaville, Canada - Boys bought fire crakers.

Saw the speaking contest at night.

May 25 Friday

Dody should finish her insulin treatments (60) [That last sentence was outlined)

Attend F.F.A. meeting in morning took boys to Plattsburgh then to Ansable Chasma - then white face mnt. then back to Malone. Took from 1:00 P.M. to 10:30 P.M. Boys were very impressed with view from top of Mnt. Car heated up going up + brakes got hot on way down.

Al Shotwell + his boys drove along with us.

May 26, Saturday

Attended F.F.A. Meeting all day.

Bangquet + night - Harlin + Ned received the Empire farmer award - Harlin given and award by the N.Y.S. Banker Assoc.

Left Marone 9:30 P.M. arrived in Cazenovia at 3:00 A.M.

Drove all night so I could be sure + see Dody.

May 27 Sunday

Church - Hospital to see Dody - She was in middle of sleep treatment. It scared me to see her like that. Dr. feel this will make her well. Saw Millie Brown + spoke to her.

May 28 Monday

School

Wampsville to County Agr teachers meeting 5:00 to 12:00 P.M. To darn late - Awfully tired.

May 29 Tuesday

Met with new veterans.

Took kids to Syracuse after school got new shoes for each.

Saw Mr. Faff of the Pomeroy Agway about borrowing some money. Said he would be out Thur. or Fri. to look at house.

May 30 Wednesday

No school.

Finished moving from Mles. in morning.

Cleaned up shingles + roofing around our house.

Friday June 1, 1951

Supper with Grma Kate.

Saturday June 2, 1951

Work with Dan De Lorme on our house all day.

Dinner with Gma Kate

Supper with my folks

Warena has been sick all week

Called Hosp. Dody awake + doing well. This news made me feel good. I hope + pray this is the turning point + that she will be home soon.

Sunday June 3, 1951

Church - Hospital to see Dody. found her very well + looking fine. She has been awake since last Mon. I wish I had gone to see her last Wed. The nurses think she is doing well.

Monday June 4, 1951

A very confused day. Had all the schools from County here for an F.F.A. Convention! My boys won the judging Contest.

Tired after it was over.

Tuesday June 5, 1951

Vet. Class.

Wednesday June 6, 1951

Worked on house after supper.

Planted some garden.

Warena, Dick and Gr.ma Kate went to see Dody. Found her well. I was worried all day for fear something would happen.

Gr.ma Kate quite pleased with herself.

Thursday June 7, 1951

Painted trim on house after supper.

Took a load of things to camp for folks.

Folks have been working like mad to get "little camp" ready for Paul Everts + family to move in for a couple of weeks.

Warena ran over "Guss" + killed her. Tip Buried her.

Friday, June 8, 1951

Painted trim + windows again tonight.

A hard week at school. Kids are excited about the end of school - hard to get along with.

Saturday, June 9, 1951

Took Soph Class to Highland Park. Everyone Had a good time.

Mr. + Mrs. Merril Baily + children went along.

Our kids practiced at church for Childrens Day. Then went to camp. Everyone home early as Chuck was to call from Texas at 7:00PM. He did call.

Sunday, June 10, 1951

Childrens Day very nice. Hospital to see Dody - she is still "holding up". Not quite as cheerful as last Sun. Hope + pray she continues to gain.

Miss her very much.

Wednesday June 13, 1951

Bobby + I went to Hospital to see Dody. She was quite well + we had a nice visit.

Saturday, June 16, 1951

Worked on weed sprayer at ?Holmes? all day - had diner with them.

folks - Barb + Dick went to camp for day + night. .

Sunday, June 17, 1951

Church - Hosp to see Dody - she is still pretty good - had a nice visit.

Monday, June 18, 1951

Ry. Regents - went to Morrisville - Ryr teachers marked papers.

Tuesday, June 19, 1951

Worked at Holmes - Again - Got sprayer running + it works. Wonderfull

Vet Class to Loinell Mathews.

Wednesday, June 20, 1951

Hosp to see Dody. Found her started under sleep treatment. She ran away from hospital and went to Airport. If only she hadn't done this. Now no one will trust her for a long time. I had so much hoped she was on her way home. Dr. Nasholdtk asked me to get some "Dial" ampules. I searched Rome. finally a drug salesman said he would have them airmailed to Drugstore in Caz. Dr. is anxious to get it so Dody can be kept sleeping.

Thursday, June 21, 1951

Waited all day for medicine but it did not come.

Talked to Henry Kaiser about loan for house. He said that if Dad gave a mortgage on his house for $5,000 I could have if dad is willing. I hoped I wouldn't have to ask any one for help. Dick is going to stand the lumber Co. bill for a while.

Midge called. Wants Barbie for a while.

Phil had his first hair cut.

Fireplace and chimney started on our house.

Tip & I wired wiring our house.

Friday, June 22, 1951

Medicine came at 8:00 a.m., took kids to school and then went to hospital. Dr. Nasholdtk said Dody was asleep & all signs were good. She wanted me to have money (10.00) for penicillin for protection against phneumonia.

Dody is to sleep for 14 days. I pray that this is what we have been waiting these 10 months for.

Got $5000 from bank. Start a special account at Caz, bank with it.

Saturday June 232, 1951

Worked around house all day. Took Barb to camp.

Class night.

Kinda lonesome after class night. Drove around - stopped visited with Gr. Kate - stayed to late - (11:30 PM)

Sunday June 24, 1951

Didn't get up until 9:00 AM - Church - Feel very lost. First Sun. that I haven't seen Dody. Pray she is sleeping + getting well. Love her + miss her.

Monday June 25, 1951

Commencement

Rod and Dick spent summer painting windows, rf--Grandpa Grime, Jack Baer's home, Webster's, Jasse's, and Mafield's. Did considerable work on parsonage and church.
Barb spent summer in Madison, Conn. with Mrs. Bill Davis' mother and step father, Mr. And Mrs. Alvin Bowman - doing housework. We visited her one weekend.
Dick to summer school evenings to make up a course in Electrical Engineering he failed.
Sept. Barb senior at Lake Erie College. Dick junior at Clarkson.
Dec. 26 Jim, Phil, Rod, and I took Grandma Virgo to Jim's in Fafax, S.C. where Glenn and family met us. They took her on to Louisiana with them for part f the winter
1964 Barb graduated from Lake Erie College - went to Cleveland and lived in boarding house with Mr. Zill and looked for work. Brought her home in July.
Summer Dick and Rod painted houses all summer. Mrs. Thomas', the Hughes, part f Dunleyer, the Hills, and Wefalls in Delphi Falls.
Dick to summer camp at Indian Town Gap for six weeks (poison ivy)
Fall Barb to work in Admission's Ofice at Syracuse - has an apartment with Gail Curtis.
Dick to last year at Clarkson
Phil freshman - out for track and hockey
Kept Jim home from school for 1 more year
1965 Dick graduated from Clarkson - went to Ft. Belvoir for summer. Spent time in hospital with poison ivy.
Phil doing very poorly at home and school - hates everyone including self.
Jim started kindergarten - loves it.
Barb moved to Washington, DC - an apartment with 2 other girls
1966 Dick to Korea in the Army Engineers as a 2nd Lt.
Barb in Washington working for Dr. West connected with Washington colleges. Has an apartment by hersf . (We spent Christmas with her. Had one chair in the whole apartment)
Phil ft home without telling anyone. Ended up at Barb's. A big mess.
Jim in 1st grade, doing very well.
1967 Phil started school at Hawthorne. Never did very well, lives with Barb in her new apartment on F Street. 1741 F. Street.
Dick signed up for another year with the Army. Sent to Vietnam in January. Was made 1st Lieutenant.
Barb went to work for the American Psychological Association at much more money than she was getting. Phil is her big worry.
Christmas in Virginia with Glenn Virgo's
1968 Dick made captain and home from the wars. He was put on a hospital testing program. Was awarded the Army Commendation Medal for his work in this area.
Barb was home 3 times this year, once this summer, once at Thanksgiving, and again at Christmas. Flew each time.
Dick mustered out f Army and fter a month f getting used to civilian fe went to work with the Millers. Found an apartment in Albany. Barb went there at Christmas time to help him furnish it.
Phil fractured his skull and was in George Washington Hospital for 10 days. He spent the summer in Texas with Paul deSantis. Paul was sent to Vietnam and Phil came home to start his junior year here.
Rented house for summer
1969 Dick still with Miller's rat farm. He found a very nice home in Voorheesville where he rents a room.
Barb is going with Jim Hobbs.
Phil is taking his senior year in half a year. Finished in January. Went to work at Cook's discount store. He left home again when we made an issue of having Leslie in his room. So they are now living together - really solved that one.
July We bought a Scamper trailer and Jim, Rod and I camped to Cafornia to visit Bobbi and George.
Rented camp for 3 weeks. Rented house to Websters for six weeks.
1970 August 22 Barbara Jeanne Virgo married to James Hobbs at Cacapon State Park, Berkley, W. VA.
Rented house to a young couple going to the University. Exchanged camps with Bill and Beth O'Brien. We went to theirs at Martha's Vineyard for a week. They had ours.
1971 Bought new blue ford Tarino
Rented camp one week to Clarks and one week to friends f theirs
Dody had D&C in June. Laid up 2 weeks
Oct. To Washington to visit Barb and Jim. A beauful fall. No frost ‘til November.
Nov. Road having a bad time with his back
Dick to work for Bog-Warner at Morris Chain. Works in Ithaca. Really likes it

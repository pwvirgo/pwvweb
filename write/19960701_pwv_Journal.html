Phil Virgo Journal for July 1996

July 9,1996

Yesterday was the first day in over a year that I slept in a bed. For the past year I have slept mostly on a small junky piece of foam rubber in a corduroy sack.

It is a luxury to have a nice queen size bed! No sheets, and haven't cleaned the blankets in that time. Sometimes I sleep out in the back yard and watch the stars. Yesterday Janice left for a month living at the dorm at gw law school, so I get to sleep in the bed. It is a luxury to have a nice queen size bed.

Sony Bono senator from CA is talking to the national press club. How he got into politics... He settled in Palm Springs and started a restaurant. He wanted to get a sign, and it was the hardest thing he ever did. He eventually resolved that the only way to get the sign was to run for mayor and fire the person he was dealing with.

July 10,1996

Got a new computer at work, a 133 mhz Pentium from Compact, spent the day setting it up. Spoke with Pablo and Joshua and Elisabeth. Pablo is planning to take a train to Syracuse this weekend to go to a concert with Mat Crup. Josh could be dropped off at the metro and come here for the weekend. No one knows how to locate Janice (who promised she would leave the paid bills on the computer desk, but instead took them with her, as always. The Toyota Previa payment is 20 days behind and we have so little money I don't want to pay 2 times. I have no way to figure out if she has sent a check)

A doctor on the Diane Rheim Show (WAMU radio) is saying that studies show that prayers are effective in healing. Controlled studies show that rats that are prayed for heal faster than the rats that aren't prayed for. Also people can pray for rats to heal more slowly - that also works. The rat studies are meant to show that this is not just positive thinking on the part of the subject. I have a problem with prayer. Don't know what God is, what I am praying to.
